Who We Are

LA Luxury Car Rental was founded in Beverly Hills with one purpose — to give clients access to the finest exotic and luxury vehicles in the world with a level of service that matches the cars themselves.

Our vehicle guarantee means you receive the exact model you book. We send photos of your specific car before your rental date so there are never any surprises.

Today we operate from 30+ locations across California and the Western United States, with concierge delivery to hotels, airports, and private addresses available throughout the service area.
